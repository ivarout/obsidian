#recipe 

## Ingredients for about 3 portions

- 120 gr okonomiyaki flour
- 150 ml water
- Cabbage 300 gr

### Optional

- Benishouga
- Nagaimo shredded
- Mochi 2 blocks cut small
- Some corn (75 gr maybe)
- Saucage (as much as you like
- Ooba 
- Negi
- Agedama

### Toppings

- Mayo
- Okonomiyaki Sauce
- Karashi
- Katsuobushi
- Aonori
